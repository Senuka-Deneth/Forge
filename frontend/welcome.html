Features

Everything on one chart. Nothing you didn't ask for.

No tab farms, no indicator soup. Three instruments, tuned to work together.

Live structure

A WebSocket stream renders every tick onto candles with EMA, RSI and MACD computed locally — support and resistance track the swings as they form.

Pivot engine

Six calculation methods — traditional to Camarilla — drawn per period across history, with per-level control over color, width and labels.

AI second opinion

A model reads the same data you do and answers with a bias, a trade plan and its reasoning — every prediction scored later for accuracy, wins and losses included.

How it works

Three steps between you and a decision.

01 — Connect

Pick a pair

Sign in and choose any market pair. Four thousand candles of history load in one call, then the live stream takes over.

02 — Read

See the structure

Pivot levels and swing structure draw themselves onto the chart. Toggle what you need, hide what you don't.

03 — Decide

Check your bias

Ask for the AI read: direction, entry zone, stop and targets — then hold it to account on the accuracy board.
